Scott Morken

Position: GUI Designer / Microcontroller Programmer

Program: Computer Engineering Technology

Biography: Scott is from Grande Prairie Alberta, and has been living in Victoria since 1993. He spent 7 years gaining general workforce experience after graduating high school in 1996. He worked as a dishwasher/prep cook, yacht detailer, salesman and computer technician during this time. He also completed the Adult Correctional Officer training program at the Justice institute of BC.

Before entering college Scott had an interest in computers and other technical things, and he actively worked to develop skills in these fields. He has worked on such projects as PHP-MySQL based websites, WxWidgets GUIs, and a 3d OpenGL real-time rocket simulator. In 2004 Scott entered the Electronics Engineering Technology program at Camosun College, and he began to get a deeper understanding of the subjects he is interested in. He gained experience building C#.NET applications in his 2 co-op terms with Natural Resources Canada's Carbon Accounting Team at the Pacific Forestry Centre. Now he plans to continue his studies at UVIC, aiming for a degree in Computer Engineering.
