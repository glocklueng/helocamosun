Michael Ghazi

Position: RF System Designer / GUI Design Assistant / Microcontroller Programmer / Webmaster

Program: Computer Engineering Technology

Biography: Michael was born in Flin Flon, MB and lived there until the age of 18. He then left town to pursue several life paths, and eventually found himself at the Canadian Forces Recruiting Centre in Winnipeg. For the six subsequent years he served with the Forces as a Naval Communicator. After his first two contracts he decided a change was needed, and retired from his life at sea.

Since his time in the Navy, Michael has been a student at Camosun College studying Computer Engineering Technology. He plans to enter the field in a government position, or in a defense contracting firm.
