Welcome to Team Heli Cam Ltd's project site for "Airwulf", an autonomous helicopter. The developers of this project are in their final quarter at Victoria BC's Camosun College. They are part of the Electronics/Computer Engineering Technology program. This diploma program runs over the course of about three years, and covers many aspects of electronic and computer engineering. In the final quarter of the program students participate in a complete design project. From concept, design, testing and production the students control all aspects of the project.

This is the official website for the project. In the navigation bar to the left you will find links to the various pages included here. Read the developers' blog to stay up to date about the progress of the project. Along the botton of the page are links to short biographies of each of the developers.

Enjoy the site, and we look forward to seeing you at out project demonstration in mid to late June.
